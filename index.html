Greco Persian War!

During this war the two sides were the Persians who wanted to rule Greece after Athens helped one of their cites and the Delian League led by Athens who just wanted to survive. This war started when Ilos was helped by Athens to get away from the Persian Empire but then caused them to come after all of Greece so the Polis banded together to fend them away.



The Persians tried to sway us with kingship but we knew siding with the Delian League was the right way to survive and stick with the Greek people. We expect freedom in the end and to keep good relations with other citys.
